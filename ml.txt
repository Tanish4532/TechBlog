        <h2><em>What is Machine Learning?</em></h2>
        <p>
          Machine learning is a method of data analysis that automates
          analytical model building. It is a branch of artificial intelligence
          based on the idea that systems can learn from data, identify patterns
          and make decisions with minimal human intervention.
        </p>
        <p>
          Machine learning is the study of computer algorithms that improve
          automatically through experience and by the use of data. It is seen as
          a part of artificial intelligence.
        </p>
        <hr />
        <h2><em>Machine Learning used for:</em></h2>
        <p>
          Machine learning is used in internet search engines, email filters to
          sort out spam, websites to make personalised recommendations, banking
          software to detect unusual transactions, and lots of apps on our
          phones such as voice recognition.
        </p>
        <hr />
        <h2><em>Four Types of Machine Learning:</em></h2>

          <li>Un-supervised learning</li>
          <li>Semi-supervised learning</li>
          <li>Reinforcement learning</li>
        </ol>
